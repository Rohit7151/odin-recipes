Sandwhich Recipe

Description

A sandwich is a dish typically consisting of vegetables, sliced cheese or meat, placed on or between slices of bread, or more generally any dish wherein bread serves as a container or wrapper for another food type.

The sandwich began as a portable, convenient finger food in the Western world, though over time it has become prevalent worldwide. There has been a social media debate over the precise definition of sandwich, and specifically whether a hot dog or open sandwich can be categorized as such.
